Natural gestures to control your devices

Turn on your lamps by pointing at them. Face your TV and drag down to reduce the volume or drag right to fast forward. Do a ‘shush gesture’ to instantly mute every device in your home. Our Gesture Dictionary was designed to be intuitive so you don’t have remember it.

Connect seamlessly with partner APIs and smart plugs

We’ve integrated with dozens of products, including Chromecast, Apple TV, Hue Lights, Bose speakers, and Dyson fans. For devices that aren’t smart, you can conveniently connect them using most popular smart plugs.

The smartest camera ever made

Recognizes people and their gestures
Our real-time Gesture Recognition Pipeline (GRP) uses motion tracking, pose estimation, 3D reconstruction, and deep learning to identify people, poses, and gestures at lightning speed.
Understands relationships between gestures and devices
Our Device Localization Pipeline (DLP) uses Semantic Segmentation and Object Tracking to monitor connected devices in 3D space, and couples that information with GRP to trigger device actions.
